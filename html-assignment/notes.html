These are my 5 tags

ishu
Defines headings, with <h1> being the largest and <h6> the smallest.
<p>
Defines a paragraph.
<img>
Embeds an image into the webpage.
<a>
Creates a hyperlink to another webpage.
<ul>, <ol>, <li>
Creates unordered or ordered lists.
